About Bihar

Bihar, a state in eastern India, is rich in history and cultural heritage. It is home to ancient sites, religious landmarks, and vibrant festivals.

Main Attractions

  • Mahabodhi Temple, Bodh Gaya
  • Nalanda University
  • Vikramshila Ruins
  • Patna Sahib Gurudwara
  • Valmiki National Park

Local Cuisine

Bihari cuisine is known for its simplicity and delicious flavors. Must-try dishes include Litti Chokha, Sattu Paratha, and Chana Ghugni.

Culture and Festivals

Bihar celebrates various festivals with great fervor, including Chhath Puja, Sonepur Mela, and Rajgir Mahotsav.

How to Reach

Bihar can be accessed by air through Patna Airport, by train via major railway stations like Patna Junction, and by road through national highways.

Adventure Tourism

Adventure enthusiasts can enjoy activities like white-water rafting in the Ganges, trekking in the Rajgir hills, and wildlife safaris in Valmiki National Park.

Wildlife

Bihar is blessed with diverse wildlife, including Bengal tigers, elephants, deer, and various bird species, found in its national parks and sanctuaries.
